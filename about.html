Our Story

Founded in 2023, Orill Exim LLP has grown into a trusted name in tissue manufacturing, wholesale, and exports. Our core mission is to provide products that blend comfort, hygiene, and consistency for homes and businesses.

Every product line is developed using quality materials and efficient manufacturing practices under the guidance of experienced professionals. Our key range includes fluffy printed tissue napkins and soft tissue paper formats built for retail and hospitality requirements.
