Our Story

Founded in 2023, Orill Exim LLP has emerged as a trusted leader in the tissue manufacturing industry. Specializing in manufacturing, exporting, wholesaling, and retailing, we offer a diverse range of premium products, including 16x16cm Fluffy Printed Tissue Napkins, 40x40cm Fluffy Printed Tissue Napkins, and Soft Tissue Napkin Paper.

Our products are crafted using top-grade materials and advanced technology, adhering to industry standards under the guidance of our skilled team. Renowned for their quality, they are in high demand worldwide, driving our rapid growth.

Under the visionary leadership of our CEO, Mr. Yash Trambadia, our success is fueled by his exceptional management skills and motivation. His ability to navigate challenges has positioned us as a key player in the market.
